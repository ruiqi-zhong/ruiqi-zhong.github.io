Research

During my PhD, I pioneered two lines of research.

Scalable Oversight

As humans, how do we supervise "super-human" AI systems that do things we can't do? How to prevent them from "pretending to do the right thing"? Checkout my slides and video.

Explaining Complex Objects

As humans, we are not good at understanding complex objects like datasets and AI systems. Can AI systems help us explain them? Checkout my video, thesis, and github.
